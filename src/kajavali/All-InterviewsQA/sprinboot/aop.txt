pointcut:
------------- 
is an expression that matches join points to determine when advice should be executed.
 Pointcuts are made up of two parts: a signature and a pointcut expression
===========
jointpoint
==================
is a point in a program's execution, such as when a method is executed or an exception is handled.
==========
advice
===========
 is a function, method, or procedure that modifies other functions when they are run at a specific join point in a program.
====================
